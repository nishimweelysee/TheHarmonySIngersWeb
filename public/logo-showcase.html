How to Use These Logos

In HTML

Embed directly in your HTML:

<img src="/ths-logo.svg" alt="THS Logo">

In CSS

Use as background image:

background-image: url('/ths-logo.svg');

In Laravel Blade

Use Laravel's asset helper:

<img src="{{ asset('ths-logo.svg') }}">

As Favicon

Add to your HTML head:

<link rel="icon" href="/ths-favicon.svg">
